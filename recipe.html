Garlic Butter Steak Bites

A quick, savory, and tender beef dinner ready in minutes.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 10 mins
Servings: 4

Description

These bite-sized pieces of sirloin steak are seared to crisp perfection in a hot skillet and tossed in a rich, savory garlic butter sauce. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or an appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Sirloin steak, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/3 cup Butter, melted
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Pat the steak cubes dry with paper towels. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over high heat until smoking.
  3. Add the steak bites in a single layer. Do not overcrowd the pan (cook in batches if necessary).
  4. Sear the steak for 2 minutes without moving it to get a good crust. Flip and cook for another 2 minutes until browned.
  5. Reduce heat to medium. Add the butter and minced garlic to the skillet. Toss the steak bites in the melting garlic butter for about 1 minute.
  6. Remove from heat, garnish with fresh parsley, and serve immediately.
